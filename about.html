About Me

Hi! I'm Kathleen

I began my career in the floral industry more than three decades ago, starting with my first job in high school and continuing today as a wholesale buyer for a local, family-run firm. Through the years I also have honed my skills as a designer for commercial and personal clients, creating mixed bouquets, centerpieces, wedding packages, and merchandising campaigns.

My background also includes extensive experience with national retailers that allowed me to develop a keen sense of color and proportion that I would love to bring to your home. Most recently, I have expanded into producing workshops and design demonstrations that focus on simple applications so participants can showcase their own style and creativity. My goal is to make the beautiful world of flowers accessible to all.
